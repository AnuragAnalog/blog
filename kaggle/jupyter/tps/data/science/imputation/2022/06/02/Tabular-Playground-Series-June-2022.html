Imputation

Approach - 1

Using Central Tendency Measures to impute the data(mean, median)

for col in null_cols:
    imputer = SimpleImputer(missing_values=np.nan, strategy='mean')

    data[col] = imputer.fit_transform(data[col].values.reshape(-1, 1))
